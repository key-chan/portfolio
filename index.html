About

Sometimes the smallest things can make all the difference in the world. We are excited to tell you about Snapit Fasteners. This is a product that replaces all those unsightly, corroding and rust - bleeding stainless steel fasteners. Snapits blend in with your gel coat or paint and do not distract from your beautiful boat. Snapits are self lubricating and require no maintenance.

There are two different styles of Snapits, one is the Snapit Screw and the other is the Snapit Wedge. Snapits are molded of highly durable, UV resistant Celcon.
